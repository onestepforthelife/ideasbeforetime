One Phone, Two Numbers - Work-Life Balance Innovation

The Problem

In 2003, running a PCO business while working at LIC India meant juggling two phones - one for personal use, one for business. The inconvenience was obvious: carrying two devices, managing two chargers, missing calls on the phone left behind.

The solution seemed simple: why not have one phone that could handle two SIM cards?

üìç Professional Context

Role: LIC India + PCO Business Owner

Pain Point: Managing separate personal and business phone numbers

Insight: Need for work-life separation without device duplication

The Proposal

My dual SIM concept included:

  • Two active SIM cards in one device
  • Separate ringtones for each number
  • Choose which SIM for outgoing calls
  • Cost savings - one device, two connections
  • Perfect for business owners and professionals
