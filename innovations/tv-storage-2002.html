Digital Video Recording & Time-Shifted Viewing

The Vision

In 2002, while managing my PCO business and working at LIC India, I envisioned a mechanism to store TV programs for later viewing - essentially what we now know as DVR (Digital Video Recorder) technology and streaming services.

The frustration was simple: missing favorite shows due to work schedules. Why couldn't we record and watch programs at our convenience?

The Concept

My vision included:

  • Digital storage of TV broadcasts
  • Pause, rewind, and replay live TV
  • Schedule recordings of favorite shows
  • Build a personal library of content
  • Watch programs at convenient times
