Accessibility Statement

Last Updated: November 26, 2025

Our Commitment: Ideas Before Time is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We are continually improving the user experience for everyone and applying relevant accessibility standards.

1. Our Accessibility Goal

We strive to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA standards. These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible for people with disabilities and user-friendly for everyone.

2. Measures We've Taken

To improve accessibility, we have implemented:

  • Semantic HTML: Proper use of headings, lists, and landmarks
  • Alt Text: Descriptive alternative text for images
  • Keyboard Navigation: All interactive elements accessible via keyboard
  • Color Contrast: Sufficient contrast ratios for text readability
  • Responsive Design: Mobile-friendly and works across devices
  • Clear Language: Simple, understandable content
  • Consistent Navigation: Predictable and consistent layout

3. Current Accessibility Features

  • ✅ Semantic HTML5 structure
  • ✅ Responsive design for all screen sizes
  • ✅ Keyboard-accessible navigation
  • ✅ Clear heading hierarchy
  • ✅ Readable font sizes (minimum 14px)
  • ✅ High contrast text and backgrounds
  • ✅ Descriptive link text
  • ✅ Form labels and instructions

4. Known Limitations

Despite our efforts, some limitations may exist:

  • Some older content may not meet current standards
  • Third-party embedded content may have accessibility issues
  • Some images may lack comprehensive alt text
  • Complex data visualizations may be challenging for screen readers

We are actively working to address these limitations.

5. Assistive Technologies

This website is designed to be compatible with:

  • Screen readers (JAWS, NVDA, VoiceOver)
  • Screen magnification software
  • Speech recognition software
  • Keyboard-only navigation
  • Browser accessibility features

6. Browser Compatibility

This website works best with current versions of:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Microsoft Edge
  • Safari

7. Personal Context

The creator of this website, Amit Kumar Agrawal, lives with Muscular Dystrophy (40% disability). This personal experience deeply informs our commitment to accessibility and inclusive design. We understand firsthand the importance of accessible digital experiences.

8. Feedback and Contact

We welcome your feedback on the accessibility of Ideas Before Time. If you encounter accessibility barriers:

  • Email: onestepforthelife@gmail.com
  • Subject Line: "Accessibility Feedback"
  • Include: Description of the issue, page URL, assistive technology used

We aim to respond to accessibility feedback within 5 business days.

9. Formal Complaints

If you are not satisfied with our response, you may file a complaint with:

  • Your local disability rights organization
  • Relevant government accessibility authority in your jurisdiction

10. Ongoing Improvements

Accessibility is an ongoing effort. We are committed to:

  • Regular accessibility audits
  • User testing with people with disabilities
  • Staff training on accessibility best practices
  • Incorporating accessibility into our development process
  • Staying updated with WCAG guidelines

11. Third-Party Content

Some content on this website is provided by third parties (embedded videos, social media links, payment processors). We cannot guarantee the accessibility of third-party content but encourage providers to maintain accessibility standards.

12. Technical Specifications

Accessibility of Ideas Before Time relies on the following technologies:

  • HTML5
  • CSS3
  • JavaScript (progressive enhancement)
  • ARIA (Accessible Rich Internet Applications) attributes where appropriate

13. Assessment and Testing

This website has been assessed using:

  • Automated accessibility testing tools
  • Manual keyboard navigation testing
  • Color contrast analyzers
  • Screen reader testing (ongoing)

Last Assessment Date: November 26, 2025

14. Continuous Improvement

We review and update this accessibility statement regularly. Our goal is to achieve and maintain WCAG 2.1 Level AA conformance across all pages.

Thank you for visiting Ideas Before Time. Your feedback helps us create a more accessible experience for everyone.
