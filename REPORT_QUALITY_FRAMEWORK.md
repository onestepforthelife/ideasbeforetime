## ğŸ” DEPTH DIMENSIONS (How Much Detail)

### DEPTH LEVEL 1: OVERVIEW (For Global/Regional)
- **What:** Basic numbers, percentages
- **Example:** "Global market: $500M, 6% CAGR"
- **Use for:** Global market, regional splits, international players

### DEPTH LEVEL 2: ANALYSIS (For Key Topics)
- **What:** Numbers + trends + drivers
- **Example:** "Global market: $500M, 6% CAGR, driven by pharma demand growth"
- **Use for:** Applications, competitive landscape, pricing trends

### DEPTH LEVEL 3: STRATEGIC INSIGHT (For India)
- **What:** Numbers + trends + drivers + opportunities + risks + recommendations
- **Example:** "India market: $50M, 8% CAGR, 70% import dependent. Opportunity: Local production can save 15% on landed cost. Risk: Raw material availability. Recommendation: Partner with EO/PO suppliers."
- **Use for:** India market, strategic recommendations, action plans

## ğŸ¯ THE "5W + 1H" FRAMEWORK

**For EVERY major topic, answer:**

### 1. WHAT (Breadth)
- What is the market size?
- What are the applications?
- What are the products/grades?

### 2. WHERE (Breadth)
- Where is it produced? (countries, companies)
- Where is it consumed? (regions, industries)
- Where is India in this? (position, dependency)

### 3. WHO (Breadth)
- Who are the global players?
- Who are the India players?
- Who are the buyers?

### 4. HOW MUCH (Depth)
- How much volume? (MT, growth)
- How much value? (USD, pricing)
- How much capacity? (by company)
- How much import? (India dependency)

### 5. WHY (Depth)
- Why is market growing? (drivers)
- Why price is changing? (factors)
- Why India imports? (local constraints)
- Why opportunity exists? (gaps)

### 6. HOW (Depth - Strategic)
- How to enter market? (strategy)
- How to reduce costs? (optimization)
- How to compete? (positioning)
- How to mitigate risks? (actions)
