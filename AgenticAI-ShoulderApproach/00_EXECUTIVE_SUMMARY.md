## 🚨 Critical Gaps Identified

### Gap 1: No Validation of Pain Point
**Issue:** Assumes US plumbers lose money on missed calls  
**Risk:** What if they already have solutions?  
**Fix:** Run 5-10 customer discovery calls BEFORE building

### Gap 2: $1000/mo Ad Spend Without Testing
**Issue:** Recommends $1000/mo from Day 1  
**Risk:** Could burn $3000-5000 before finding PMF  
**Fix:** Start with $300/mo, test messaging, scale what works

### Gap 3: Agentic AI is Oversimplified
**Issue:** Shows 2-question qualification flow  
**Risk:** Real qualification needs 5-7 questions + objection handling  
**Fix:** Build complete qualification script with branching logic

### Gap 4: No Mention of GHL Learning Curve
**Issue:** Assumes you can build snapshots immediately  
**Risk:** GHL has steep learning curve (2-4 weeks)  
**Fix:** Budget 2 weeks for GHL mastery before launch

### Gap 5: No Existing Asset Leverage
**Issue:** Doesn't leverage Ideas Before Time site or SPO experience  
**Risk:** Starting from zero when you have existing credibility  
**Fix:** Use your site as proof of automation expertise
