### 3. Technical Specifications

**Authentication:**
- Phone number + OTP
- Biometric login (Face ID, Touch ID, Fingerprint)
- JWT tokens (access + refresh)
- Two-factor authentication (business owner app)

**Permissions:**
- Location (GPS navigation, tracking)
- Camera (job photos, profile photos)
- Phone (make calls)
- Notifications (push notifications)
- Storage (save photos, cache data)

**Offline Functionality:**
- View cached data (jobs, history, dashboard)
- Add notes (sync when online)
- Cannot: Book new jobs, make payments, send messages

**Push Notifications:**
- Technician: New job, job reminder, customer message, payment received
- Customer: Booking confirmation, technician en route, service completed, review request
- Business Owner: Critical alerts, daily summary, weekly report, negative review
