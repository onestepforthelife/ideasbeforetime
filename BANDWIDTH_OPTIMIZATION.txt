ADDITIONAL OPTIMIZATIONS (OPTIONAL):
=====================================

If you want even faster loading:

1. IMAGE OPTIMIZATION (Recommended)
   - Compress images to 80% quality
   - Convert to WebP format (50% smaller)
   - Use: https://squoosh.app/ (free online tool)
   
2. MINIFY FILES (Optional)
   - Minify HTML/CSS/JS (remove spaces)
   - Use: https://www.minifier.org/
   - Saves ~20-30% file size

3. LAZY LOADING (Optional)
   - Add loading="lazy" to images
   - Images load only when visible
   - Faster initial page load
