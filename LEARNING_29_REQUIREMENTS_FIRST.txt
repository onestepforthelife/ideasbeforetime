## ðŸ“‹ WHAT TO DOCUMENT

### 1. Requirements (What Must Work)
- List 3-5 clear requirements
- Be specific and measurable
- Example: "No email popup appears"

### 2. Anti-Requirements (What Must NOT Work)
- List things that should NOT happen
- Example: "Must NOT open email client"

### 3. Test Checklist
- 3-5 test scenarios maximum
- Step-by-step instructions
- Expected results for each step

### 4. Expected Behavior
- Exact outputs for each scenario
- Example: "Should see: 'Instant Access Granted!'"

### 5. Success Criteria
- When is it considered done?
- Clear pass/fail criteria
