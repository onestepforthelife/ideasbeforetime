No Database Storage: We do not maintain a database of user information.

Session Data: Browser session storage is used temporarily and cleared when you close your browser.

4. Your Rights (DPDP Act 2023 Compliance)

Under India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request what data we have about you (email us)
  • Correction: Request correction of any inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data at any time
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for data processing
  • Data Portability: Request your data in a portable format

To exercise these rights, email: onestepforthelife@gmail.com

7. Cookies and Tracking

Note: Since we don't store user data in databases, breach risk is minimal.
