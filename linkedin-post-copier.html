📋 LinkedIn Post Copier

Easy tool to copy all your LinkedIn posts at once

📝 How to Use:

  1. Go to your LinkedIn profile page
  2. Scroll down to "Activity" section
  3. Click "Show all activity" or "See all posts"
  4. Scroll down to load more posts (keep scrolling until you see all)
  5. Select ALL text on the page (Ctrl+A or Cmd+A)
  6. Copy (Ctrl+C or Cmd+C)
  7. Paste below and click "Process Posts"
