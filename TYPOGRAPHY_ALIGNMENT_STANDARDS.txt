PROPOSED STANDARDS:
===================

### FONT SIZE HIERARCHY:

**Headings:**
- H1: 2.5rem (40px) - Page title
- H2: 2rem (32px) - Section headings
- H3: 1.5rem (24px) - Subsection headings
- H4: 1.25rem (20px) - Card titles
- H5: 1.125rem (18px) - Small headings
- H6: 1rem (16px) - Smallest headings

**Body Text:**
- Body: 1rem (16px) - Main content
- Small: 0.875rem (14px) - Captions, metadata
- Tiny: 0.75rem (12px) - Fine print

**Navigation:**
- Logo: 1.25rem (20px)
- Links: 0.9375rem (15px)

**Footer:**
- Links: 0.875rem (14px)
- Copyright: 0.8125rem (13px)

### ALIGNMENT STANDARDS:

**Page Container:**
- Max-width: 1200px (standard)
- Margin: 0 auto (centered)
- Padding: 20px (sides)

**Content Sections:**
- Default: Left-aligned
- Hero sections: Center-aligned
- Footer: Center-aligned
- Forms: Left-aligned labels, centered buttons

**Text Alignment:**
- Headings: Left-aligned (unless hero)
- Paragraphs: Left-aligned (better readability)
- Buttons: Center-aligned in containers
- Navigation: Space-between (logo left, links right)
