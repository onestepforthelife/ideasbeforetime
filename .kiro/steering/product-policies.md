  1. Tool is free (no product being sold)

```
"₹21 covers backend API costs and support infrastructure.

```

- Backend API costs (OpenAI, Gemini)
- Server infrastructure
- Support & maintenance

Since service is delivered instantly and costs are incurred immediately, 
refunds are not applicable. Questions? Email: onestepforthelife@gmail.com
