Index Risk/Opportunity Segment Impact Level Mitigation/Strategy

6.2 100% Import Dependency - VAM Acrylic Polymers HIGH Long-term contracts with Celanese/Wacker, inventory buffer (45-60 days), pricing pass-through clauses
6.3 Fatty Alcohol Price Volatility Surfactants MEDIUM-HIGH Hedging palm oil futures, dual sourcing (petrochemical + bio-based), flexible formulations
6.4 Chlorosilanes Technology Barrier Silicones BARRIER (Opportunity) For new entrants: Partner with oligopoly players. For incumbents: Maintain technology moat through R&D
6.5 IOCL Butyl Acrylate Capacity (150 KT, July 2025) Acrylic Polymers GAME-CHANGER Opportunity: Shift from 60 KT deficit to 80 KT surplus. Expected 15-20% price reduction, ₹4,000 Cr forex savings
6.6 Acrylic Hybrids Import Dependency (65-70%) Acrylic Polymers OPPORTUNITY Domestic capacity utilization only 20-30%. Investment opportunity in high-margin (40-50%) segment
6.7 EO Availability (MEG Sector Dominance) Surfactants MEDIUM Long-term offtake agreements with EO producers, vertical integration (India Glycols model)
6.8 Silicon Metal Price Volatility (Solar/EV Demand) Silicones MEDIUM Long-term contracts, pass-through pricing to customers (justified by performance value)
6.9 Water-borne Coatings Shift (9.89% CAGR) Acrylic Polymers MEGA-TREND Opportunity: VOC regulations driving acrylic emulsion demand. Focus on low-VOC Pure Acrylics and Hybrids
