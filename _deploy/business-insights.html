RBI barred Paytm Payments Bank from onboarding new customers due to compliance issues. Parent company Paytm scrambling to partner with other banks. Impact: Regulatory scrutiny on fintech intensifying.

Sector: Fintech | Lesson: Compliance non-negotiable in regulated sectors
