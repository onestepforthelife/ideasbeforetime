Cost:

- .in domain: ₹500-800/year (cheaper but less global)

STEP 2: CONNECT TO CLOUDFLARE PAGES
------------------------------------

1. Go to Cloudflare Pages Dashboard
2. Click your project: ideas-before-time
3. Click "Custom domains"
4. Click "Set up a custom domain"
5. Enter your domain: onestep.in
6. Cloudflare will show DNS records

STEP 3: UPDATE DNS RECORDS
---------------------------

Add these records at your domain registrar:

Type: CNAME
Name: @
Value: ideas-before-time.pages.dev
TTL: Auto

Type: CNAME
Name: www
Value: ideas-before-time.pages.dev
TTL: Auto

STEP 4: WAIT FOR PROPAGATION
-----------------------------
- DNS changes take 5 minutes to 24 hours
- Usually works in 15-30 minutes
- Cloudflare will verify automatically

STEP 5: ENABLE SSL (Automatic)
-------------------------------
- Cloudflare provides free SSL
- Your site will be https://onestep.in
- Secure and professional
