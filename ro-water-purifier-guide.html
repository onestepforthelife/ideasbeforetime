✅ The 3-Point Consumer Protection Protocol

This protocol eliminates the technician's ability to cheat on performance, parts, or hygiene.

✅ Check 1: Hygiene (Biological Safety)

The Demand: Politely but firmly ask: "Before you open the machine or handle new parts, please use hand sanitizer or wear disposable gloves."

Why it Matters: Poor hygiene is the #1 cause of biological contamination (bacteria/viruses) inside your clean water tank and filters, regardless of how well the RO works.

✅ Check 2: Branding & Price (Financial Protection)

The Demand: Before installation, ask to see the new RO membrane in its packaging.

The Verification: Reject completely unbranded parts. Accept reputable OEM brands like HYDRAMEM, CSM, or Lexpure.

The Cost Control: Use the OEM price list (₹700-₹1,500 for quality membrane) to negotiate fair price. Do not pay ₹3,000 for a part that costs ₹800.

✅ Check 3: The TDS Isolation Test (Performance Guarantee)

The Scam: A technician can install a bad membrane and manipulate the TDS blending controller to show an "acceptable" final reading (150 ppm), while mixing in large amounts of raw, unpurified water.

The Test (Ultimate Defense): Demand to test the water coming directly out of the RO membrane housing (the Permeate Line) before it reaches the blending valve.

The Result: This isolated reading (TDSmem) must demonstrate a 90% or higher rejection rate of the raw inlet water (TDSin).

Example: If your Kharghar water is TDSin = 400 ppm, the membrane output (TDSmem) must be ≤ 40 ppm.

Final Output Check: Only after the membrane passes the Isolation Test should the technician adjust the blending valve to bring the final drinking water TDS to the optimal 150-300 ppm range.

🔬 Visual Flow Diagram: How RO Water Purification Works

Understanding the water path helps you know exactly where to test.

Water Purification Flow

1

Raw Inlet Water

Municipal/borewell water enters. TDSin = 50-800 ppm (Navi Mumbai)

↓
2

Pre-Filters (Sediment + Carbon)

Removes large particles, chlorine, odor. Does NOT reduce TDS significantly.

↓
3

RO Membrane (The Core)

Removes 90%+ dissolved solids. This is where TDS drops dramatically.

↓
🎯

Permeate Line (TEST HERE!)

This is where you test TDSmem for the Isolation Test. Must show ≥90% rejection.

Example: If TDSin = 400 ppm → TDSmem must be ≤ 40 ppm

↓
4

Blending Valve (TDS Controller)

Mixes small amount of raw water back to add minerals. Adjusts final TDS to 150-300 ppm.

↓
5

Final Drinking Water

Optimal TDS = 150-300 ppm. Safe, mineral-balanced water for consumption.

💡 Key Insight

By testing at the Permeate Line (Step 3), you verify the membrane is actually working—before the technician can manipulate the blending valve to hide a bad membrane!

🚫 Scam Indicators - Red Flags

Watch out for these warning signs during service:

❌ Technician refuses Isolation Test

If they won't let you test at the permeate line, they're likely hiding something.

❌ Parts without packaging/logo

Genuine OEM parts come in branded packaging with security seals.

❌ Pre-filter kit charged >₹1,500

These are cheap components. Anything above ₹750 is excessive markup.

❌ Membrane charged >₹3,000

Quality membranes cost ₹700-₹1,500. Higher prices = scam.

❌ Rushing through service

Professional service takes time. Rushing means cutting corners.
