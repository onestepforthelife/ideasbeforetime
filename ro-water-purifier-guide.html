🎯 Understanding Your Water Quality

What is TDS? TDS means how much dirt and minerals are in your water. Lower number = cleaner water.

Normal tap water has TDS between 50-150. Borewell water can be higher - up to 800.

✅ 3 Simple Checks to Protect Your Family

Do these 3 easy checks every time service person comes. Takes only 5 minutes but saves your health and money.

✅ Check 1: Clean Hands = Safe Water

What to do: Before they touch your water purifier, ask them to use hand sanitizer or wear gloves.

Why: Dirty hands put germs in your water tank. Your family can get sick even if the purifier works.

What to say: "Please clean your hands before touching the water parts."

✅ Check 2: See the Box, Check the Price

What to do: Before they install any new part, ask to see it in its box with brand name.

Good brands: HYDRAMEM, CSM, Vontron, Lexpure, Shapure, Kent, Aquaguard

Price check: Use the price table above. If they charge ₹3,000 for something that costs ₹800 on Amazon, say NO.

Smart option: Buy parts yourself from Amazon (links above) and pay them only ₹300-₹500 to install.

✅ Check 3: The Water Test (Most Important!)

The problem: Some service people install cheap parts that don't clean water properly. They hide this by mixing dirty water with clean water.

The solution: Test the water BEFORE it gets mixed. Ask them to show you the water coming directly from the cleaning part.

Simple rule: The cleaning part should remove 90% of dirt from your water.

Example: If your tap water has 400 dirt level, cleaned water should be 40 or less (90% cleaned).

What to say: "Please test the water before mixing."

If they refuse: This is a warning sign. They might be hiding something. Call someone else.

Understanding how water flows helps you know where to check.

Water Purification Flow

1

Your Tap Water Enters

Tap or borewell water comes in. Dirt level = 50-800

↓
2

First Filters

Removes big particles, smell. Does NOT reduce dirt level much.

↓
3

Main Cleaning Part

Removes 90% of dirt. This is where water becomes clean.

↓
🎯

TEST HERE!

This is where you test the water. Must show 90% cleaning.

Example: If tap water = 400 → cleaned water must be 40 or less

↓
4

Mixing Valve

Mixes small amount of tap water back to add minerals. Final level = 150-300.

↓
5

Your Drinking Water

Best level = 150-300. Safe, healthy water for your family.

💡 Important

By testing at Step 3, you check if the cleaning part is working - before they mix water to hide problems!

🚫 Warning Signs - When They Are Cheating You

If you see any of these signs, be careful. They might be trying to cheat you:

❌ Refuses to test the water

If they say "no need to test" or "trust me" - this is a warning sign. Honest service people will show you test results.

❌ Parts have no brand name or box

Real parts come in boxes with brand names. If they pull out parts from plain plastic bag - say NO.

❌ Charges more than ₹1,500 for filters

Filters cost ₹400-₹750. If they charge ₹2,000 or more, they're overcharging you.

❌ Charges more than ₹2,000 for main part

Main cleaning part costs ₹700-₹1,500. Anything above ₹2,000 is too much. Check Amazon prices (links above).

❌ Finishes in 10 minutes

Proper service takes 30-45 minutes. If they rush through in 10 minutes, they're not doing it properly.

❌ Doesn't clean hands

If they touch water parts with dirty hands, your water gets germs. Always ask them to clean hands first.
