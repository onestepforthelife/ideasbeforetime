## 🎯 DECISION POINTS

### Decision 1: Affiliate vs SaaS Mode
**Affiliate (40% commission):**
- ✅ Lower risk
- ✅ No billing management
- ❌ Lower margins

**SaaS Mode (100% revenue):**
- ✅ Higher margins
- ✅ Full control
- ❌ Handle billing/support

**Recommendation:** Start affiliate, switch to SaaS after 10 clients

### Decision 2: US LLC vs India Entity
**US LLC (Wyoming):**
- ✅ Asset protection
- ✅ Low bureaucracy ($60/year)
- ✅ Access to Mercury/Wise
- ❌ 2-4 weeks setup

**India Proprietorship:**
- ✅ Faster setup
- ❌ No asset protection
- ❌ Harder to get US payment processors

**Recommendation:** US LLC via Stripe Atlas or Doola

### Decision 3: Target Niche
**Plumbers:**
- ✅ High job value ($500-2000)
- ✅ Urgent need (leaks, emergencies)
- ❌ More competition

**Electricians:**
- ✅ High job value ($300-1500)
- ✅ Less competition
- ❌ Less urgent

**Recommendation:** Start with plumbers (validate faster)
