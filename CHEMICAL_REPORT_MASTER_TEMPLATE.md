## ğŸ¯ MANDATORY SECTIONS (Every Report Must Have)

### 1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
- Market size (global & India)
- Growth rate (CAGR)
- Key players (top 3-5)
- Critical insights (3-5 bullet points)
- Strategic recommendation (1 paragraph)

### 2. MARKET SIZE & GROWTH
- **Global Market:** Value (USD), Volume (MT), CAGR
- **India Market:** Value (USD), Volume (MT), CAGR
- **Regional Split:** Asia-Pacific, Europe, Americas, Others
- **Historical Data:** 2020-2024
- **Forecast:** 2025-2030

### 3. SUPPLY CHAIN / VALUE CHAIN
- **Raw Materials:** Sources, suppliers, pricing
- **Intermediates:** Processing steps, key players
- **End Products:** Final applications
- **Company Capacities:** Production capacity by major players
- **Flow Diagram:** Visual representation

### 4. APPLICATIONS & END USES
- **Application Split:** By industry (%, volume)
- **End-Use Sectors:** Pharma, Cosmetics, Industrial, etc.
- **Growth Drivers:** Why demand is increasing
- **Emerging Applications:** New uses, innovations

### 5. GEOGRAPHIC ANALYSIS
- **Makers (Production):** Countries, companies, capacity
- **Buyers (Consumption):** Countries, demand, growth
- **Sellers (Trade):** Export/import flows
- **India Specific:** Import vs Local production, dependencies

### 6. PRICING & COST STRUCTURE
- **Current Pricing:** USD/kg or USD/MT
- **Historical Trends:** 3-5 year price movement
- **Cost Breakdown:** Raw material %, processing %, logistics %
- **Margin Analysis:** Typical margins by segment
- **Price Drivers:** What affects pricing

### 7. COMPETITIVE LANDSCAPE
- **Global Players:** Top 5-10 companies, market share
- **India Players:** Local manufacturers, capacity
- **Competitive Positioning:** Strengths, weaknesses
- **Market Concentration:** HHI index or CR4/CR5

### 8. STRATEGIC RECOMMENDATIONS
- **Immediate Actions:** Next 3-6 months
- **Medium-term Strategy:** 6-12 months
- **Long-term Vision:** 1-3 years
- **Risk Mitigation:** Key risks and how to address
- **Opportunity Areas:** Where to focus
